Movies

My favorite movies are often a mix of science fiction, action, drama, and mind-bending concepts.

Tokyo Drift

Category: Action, Racing

Synopsis: A rebellious teenager is sent to Tokyo and discovers the underground world of drift racing.

Director: Justin Lin

Stars: Lucas Black, Sung Kang, Nathalie Kelley

Real Steel

Category: Sci-Fi, Action, Sports

Synopsis: In the near future, robot boxing is a top sport. A washed-up boxer reconnects with his son as they build and train a champion robot.

Director: Shawn Levy

Stars: Hugh Jackman, Dakota Goyo, Evangeline Lilly

Pacific Rim

Category: Sci-Fi, Action, Adventure

Synopsis: When monstrous creatures rise from the sea, humanity fights back with giant robots piloted by two-person teams.

Director: Guillermo del Toro

Stars: Charlie Hunnam, Idris Elba, Rinko Kikuchi
